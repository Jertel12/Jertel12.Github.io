PROJECT 1

A big problem in Japan today is acid rain. This is caused by air pollution emitted from nuclear power plants. When this acidic rain is precipitated it contaminates lakes and resevoirs degrading the country's water quality and harming their aquatic life. This causes a major depletion of Japans two largest economic markets, Fish and Tropical Limber.

Japan originally planned to phase out nuclear power after the Fukushima disaster in 2011. Although recently they have implemented a new policy to restart nuclear power and keep them under strict saftey regulations.
