Album Review

The final work I will showcase in this portfolio is my album review of Tha Carter V by Lil Wayne. For this assignment I had to choose an album that had recently released and that I thought was important. I selected this album because it was Lil Wayne's fifth installment of his "Tha Carter" series which had already gained immense respect in the Hip-Hop community so there was no way this one would be any different.

In this review I talk about how versitile Lil Wayne is from track to track on this Album. I also describe emotionally open Lil Wayne gets with thte listener and how this is a new side that we dont get too see too often.
